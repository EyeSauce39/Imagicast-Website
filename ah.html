Abandoned Horror

About

Abandoned Horror is a multiplayer horror game by Imagicast, that aims to be fun, interactive, and unique to its' playerbase. As well as challenge the idea of horror games, and poke a little fun at the genre. There's 1 Beast, 1 Maniac, and 6 Victims per game. The Beast's goal is to try and eliminate the Victims, the Maniac is a strategist who's goal is to outwit and torture Victims, and the Victims are hunted by them both, and must put an end to the Beast.
